Swhara Collection was born from a deep reverence for the dera — a garment that has adorned women for generations, carrying with it stories of grace, ceremony, and identity. We honour that legacy while giving it new breath.

Every piece is made with care, every detail considered. From the weight of the silk to the drape of the hem, Swhara deras are designed to make you feel as extraordinary as you are.
